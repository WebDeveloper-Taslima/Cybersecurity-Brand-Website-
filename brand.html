SECTION · 01

Logo Lockups

Wordmark + helmet-shield mark. Always paired unless the mark stands alone as a favicon or social avatar.

Do
  • · Keep clear-space equal to the mark's height around the lockup.
  • · Use the mono-dark variant on imagery or dark surfaces.
  • · Pair with Satoshi only.
Don't
  • · Recolour the mark outside the brand palette.
  • · Stretch, rotate, or add drop shadows.
  • · Place on busy photography without a scrim.
